My thought on competitive programming

Into the world of vast computer technology. Even if you learn 'A' in this year, 2 years into the future the trend will be 'B', 5 more years later and the trend is 'C'. Humans will be always hungry on knowledge, on something new. With this kond of world, we need to always learn, learn faster than the machine. Knowledge, frameworks, languages will be vary. But have the same fundamental, which is logic.

Competitive programming trained your problem solving skill and logic. Looking the same problem with differents angles and implementation. It is one of the way to improve your logic and problem solving skill. And it's also fun to try to solve a problem and participate in contests.
