Our Extracuriculer

Being a normal senior highschool student, i don't know what do i want to do, what is my purpose of learning. But honestly, in my last grade on junior high school i gain interest in programming. And surprise, surprise. One of the extracuriculer in my senior high school is called "Web Design". Although sometimes we pretty much learn random things, it's still a great place and a great community, because there are many diversity on what we learnt. Some studied game development. Some studied web development, and some learn algorithm via competitive programming.

My experience participating on student company program

Student company is a progam that's held by JA Indonesia (Junior Achievement Indonesia) with collaboration with many company as their sponsors to train senior high school student to become an entrepreneur. My school is one of five school in Surabaya that are participated in this program. 20 Students from tenth grade were chosen to participate in this program.

For me, it was an amazing experience. We get to know how to do presentation, learn entreprenurialship, learn finance, production, marketing and many business related knowledge, and we even built our own company. Our student company name is Rasendriya SC. I'm as the quality control manager and as the App developer in Rasendriya SC.
